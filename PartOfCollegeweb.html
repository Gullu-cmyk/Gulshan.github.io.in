LIGHTING MANY LIVES

The GD Rungta Educational Society started in the year 1999 with a mere initial intake of 240 students. Today, the scenario has undergone a remarkable transformation. There are more than 4000 students from different parts of the country and globe on its rolls today.

As the strength of students pursuing Engineering, Technical, Management, Dental, Graduation and Post Graduation grows, the need for more teachers and supporting staff also grows with it. Our faculty is very carefully selected by our highly professional team who are the bridge between the parents and the institution, upholding the belief of the trust in ethical practices.
